Who Are We:

Mission & Vision Statement
Our Vision: An African Uptopia where citizens have meaningful lives, and countries otherwise referred to as third world can participate in the global economy.

Our Reason For Being:
Itinere was founded with a rebellious spirit and a proud objective to offer a better way of life to the many people via better and more affordable communication tools while leading the way for socially-conscious businesses and creating opportunity.

Goals & Objectives
Itinere has a goal to create an efficient and prosperous network of merchants on the African continent. With a further goal of a B2B setting where we create new resellers and provide them all that is needed for their success and growth. A healthy, successful compnay that is a leader in customer service and that has a loyal customer following. We will accomplish this by attaining the following objectives:

- Provide stellar customer service to all of our customers.

- Constantly work to reduce logistics cost and pass those savings onto customers.

- Turn Itinere into a household name through an all inclusive culture that values every stakeholder and is always improving to meet the needs of its communities.
